Let's shape our board!

Since our brainstorm on AI in MS at the pre-evening of ECTRIMS2025, we have now transformed officially into the “international community for AI in MS” (iAIMS). All of this would not have been possible without your continued enthusiasm. Thank you!

In the last year, we achieved the following:
 1. We launched this website
 2. We created an iAIMS Discord channel to facilitate communication
 3. We hosted several journal clubs on AI methodology, trustworthiness, and the perspective of persons with MS on AI
 4. Our proposal for an educational session at MSToronto 2026 on AI for researchers and clinicians was accepted
 5. We drafted a manuscript on a guiding framework for clinically meaningful AI in MS, currently under consideration

The time has now come to establish a formal board structure to continue these efforts. We would like to suggest a two-phase process:

Phase 1: Defining board structure

Phase 1a: Feedback

Phase 1b: Voting

Voting did not start yet.

In this phase, we will ask the approval of the board structure (v2) by filling out the following Google Forms.
You can download the board structure (v2) here.

The options are:
 1. Approval without modifications
 2. Approval with modifications but no new review round
 3. No approval and need for a new review round.

This call will be closed at 17/08/2026 23:59 CET. With a minimum of 90% respondents that responded [1] or [2] out of at least 100 responses, we approve the board structure and enter phase 2. If this is not the case, we will launch a new review round which will be communicated.

Phase 2: Fill board positions

Phase 2a: Nominations

Nominations did not start yet.

The final board structure from phase 1 will be posted on this web page for you to download. We invite anyone to nominate themselves or others via a Google Forms (will be included in the web page) up until 14/09/2026 at 23:59 CET.

Phase 2b: Voting

Voting did not start yet.

Lastly, we publicly announce the candidates in the week after the end of phase 2a on this web page, after which you can vote via Google Forms (to be announced on this web page) up until 28/09/2026 at 23:59 CET. After review in the week after, we will announce the final board structure!

We are very much looking forward to your contributions. Please do not hesitate to get involved in the creation of our board, and with this shape the future of iAIMS!
